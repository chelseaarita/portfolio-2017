I AM A SOFTWARE ENGINEERING STUDENT.

Originally from the state of Hawaii, I now reside in San Francisco, California.

My background is print graphic design and studied at the Academy of Art University.

CHELSEA RUNS WITH THE BOYS INSTEAD OF CHASING THEM

I grew up liking a lot of the same things boys did, but I didn't see anything wrong with that until I heard I couldn't do something because I was a girl. I wanted to prove them wrong.

I grew up around computers so I naturally had a curiosity about them. It started off by playing games in elementary school but by high school, that's when things changed. I got the internet. I was introduced to developing and designing personal websites and was instantly hooked. I knew it was then that I wanted to become a developer.

I took a detour by majoring in graphic design, which didn't challenge me in ways that programming has. But with my design background and developing skills, I am excited to combine the two together...and to continue running with the boys.
